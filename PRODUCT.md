# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Digital creators, freelancers, solopreneurs, and AI power-users who want a competitive edge. They are self-directed, tech-literate, and motivated by revenue and efficiency. They consume content on desktop and mobile, often purchasing late at night after research. They are skeptical of generic info-products and respond to authority, specificity, and proof.

## Product Purpose

Schep Digital sells premium AI tools, prompt packs, guides, and templates on Gumroad. Every PDF product is a standalone, actionable resource that delivers immediate value. The store exists to help creators and professionals harness AI before the mainstream catches up. Success = a buyer who opens the PDF, uses it the same day, and tells someone else about it.

## Brand Personality

Bold, Expert, Modern. The brand speaks with earned authority — no hype, no fluff. It respects the reader's intelligence. Tone is direct, confident, and slightly irreverent. Think Stripe meets a elite founder who ships.

## Anti-references

- Generic Canva templates: pastel colours, decorative serif fonts, floral dividers, "digital download" energy
- Cheap ebook aesthetics: yellow highlights, Comic Sans energy, clip-art icons, 2010-era info-product design
- Overly playful / emoji-heavy: feels like a tweet thread, not a premium product
- Corporate PowerPoint: bullet-point slides, stock photo backgrounds, boardroom beige

## Design Principles

1. **Earn every pixel** — no decorative elements that don't serve comprehension or hierarchy
2. **Dark authority** — dark backgrounds signal premium and technical credibility in this niche
3. **Type as hero** — bold, oversized headings carry the weight; the grid is tight and intentional
4. **Specificity over vagueness** — concrete examples, real prompts, numbered steps — never generic advice
5. **Print-ready precision** — every page must look as good printed as on screen; no web-only tricks

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum for all text. High contrast ratios throughout (≥7:1 for body text on dark backgrounds). Avoid relying on colour alone to convey meaning. No motion in PDFs.
