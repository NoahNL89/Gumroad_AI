1. **Treat the first 0.5 seconds as the whole video.** On TikTok/Reels people decide to keep watching in about half a second. Your hook isn't the intro *to* your content — it IS your content; everything after is just proof. Cut every "hey guys, welcome back" opener and lead with the payoff.

5. **Name the exact person in the first line.** "If you're a freelancer charging by the hour, stop scrolling." The right viewer self-selects and freezes; everyone else scrolling past costs you nothing.
